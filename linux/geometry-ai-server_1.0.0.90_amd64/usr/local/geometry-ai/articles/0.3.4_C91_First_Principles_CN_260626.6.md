## 第二章 $a_1/a_0$ 的纯几何计算

### 2.1 $M(a) = S^3 \times S^3 \times S^3$ 的标量曲率

**定理 2.1（标量曲率分解）**：设三个 $S^3$ 因子分别具有半径 $r_\mathcal{M}, r_\mathcal{C}, r_\mathcal{I}$，每个 $S^3$ 配备标准 round metric。则：

$$R(S^3; r) = \frac{6}{r^2}$$

$$R_{M(a)} = R(S^3_\mathcal{M}) + R(S^3_\mathcal{C}) + R(S^3_\mathcal{I}) = 6\left(\frac{1}{r_\mathcal{M}^2} + \frac{1}{r_\mathcal{C}^2} + \frac{1}{r_\mathcal{I}^2}\right)$$

**证明：** 乘积流形的标量曲率是各因子标量曲率之和。$S^3$ 配备半径 $r$ 的 round metric 时，Ricci 曲率 $\mathrm{Ric} = 2r^{-2}g$，标量曲率 $R = \mathrm{tr}_g\mathrm{Ric} = 3 \cdot 2r^{-2} = 6/r^2$。$\blacksquare$

### 2.2 $a_1/a_0$ 的表达式

**定理 2.2（比率公式）**：

$$\frac{a_1}{a_0} = \frac{1}{r_\mathcal{M}^2} + \frac{1}{r_\mathcal{C}^2} + \frac{1}{r_\mathcal{I}^2}$$

**证明：**

$$a_0 = 16 \cdot \mathrm{Vol}(M(a)) = 16 \cdot (2\pi^2 r_\mathcal{M}^3) \cdot (2\pi^2 r_\mathcal{C}^3) \cdot (2\pi^2 r_\mathcal{I}^3) = 16 \cdot 8\pi^6 \cdot r_\mathcal{M}^3 r_\mathcal{C}^3 r_\mathcal{I}^3$$

$$a_1 = 16 \cdot \frac{1}{6} \int_{M(a)} R \, d\mathrm{vol} = 16 \cdot \frac{1}{6} \cdot \left[\frac{6}{r_\mathcal{M}^2} + \frac{6}{r_\mathcal{C}^2} + \frac{6}{r_\mathcal{I}^2}\right] \cdot \mathrm{Vol}(M(a))$$

$$= 16 \cdot \mathrm{Vol}(M(a)) \cdot \left(\frac{1}{r_\mathcal{M}^2} + \frac{1}{r_\mathcal{C}^2} + \frac{1}{r_\mathcal{I}^2}\right)$$

相除即得。$\blacksquare$

**推论 2.2a（对称极限）：** 若 $r_\mathcal{M} = r_\mathcal{C} = r_\mathcal{I} = r$，则

$$\frac{a_1}{a_0} = \frac{3}{r^2}$$
